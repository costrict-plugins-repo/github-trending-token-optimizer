## What each kind of use costs

| Who you are | What you need |
|---|---|
| Personal, hobby, research, or education | Nothing. Full source, runs locally, no purchase. |
| Small team: under 5 people OR under $20k/month revenue | A no-cost commercial license, granted automatically. Just use it. |
| Started personal, now becoming a business | Your past use is fine. A built-in 32-day grace period applies after any written notice. Reach out for a commercial license when ready. |
| Larger company or commercial use | A commercial license. Contact below. Terms are reasonable and size-appropriate. |

## Personal, hobby, research, education

Use it freely. Full source, runs entirely on your machine, no license purchase needed. That is the intended default.

## Small teams

Small teams (under 5 people, or under $20k per month in revenue) get a no-cost commercial license automatically. No paperwork, no purchase. If you want to [sponsor the project](https://github.com/sponsors/alexgreensh), that is appreciated but never required.

## Growing into commercial use

If a personal project turns into a business, past use is fine. The license has a built-in 32-day grace period after any written notice, so there is runway to sort things out. When you are ready, reach out for a commercial license.

## Larger commercial use

Companies past the small-team thresholds need a commercial license. Terms scale with company size and are designed to be reasonable. Get in touch to arrange one.

## Why source-available

Token Optimizer reads your session transcripts, configuration, and memory files. Source-available means you can audit exactly what it does before you trust it with that data. Nothing leaves your machine, and the code that proves it is open to read. For the data-handling detail, see [Your data and privacy](/reference/data-and-privacy/).
